Share dealing happens every day. Usually, there are lots of traders whose job is to get the order from clients and call the trading system and make transactions every certain time period. However, on our Trade4U website, instead of calling the trader to sell or buy stocks, clients can easily do this themselves.



To make a transaction, clients just need to input 2 parameters. One is the amount of shares they want to sell and another is the initial price of the share. The system will split the order evenly and call the simulator API to complete the job. And our Trade4U system will keep every record of every single success/failure status of each selling operations. So that when the market closes every day, clients can get a virtual result of how many shares have been successfully sold.
